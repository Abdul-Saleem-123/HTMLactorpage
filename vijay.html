Vijay

Vijay, often referred to as Thalapathy Vijay by his fans, is one of the most prominent actors in the Tamil film industry (Kollywood). Born Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, India, Vijay is the son of renowned Tamil film director S.A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar.
